BANKS

About

Jillian Rose Banks, known by her stage name Banks is a singer-songwriter from California. Her music often includes samples of her singing or humming melodies as the base since she has said she doesn't play any other instrument than piano and often uses her own vocals to write and record melodies. Her lyrics are usually very poetic and metaphorical with many intended meanings that she leaves up to the listener to decide which they identify with.
